Pesto Pasta

Description

This quick and easy pesto pasta is a delicious vegetarian weeknight dinner! I like to add a handful of arugula, but feel free to skip it or use your favorite soft greens in its place.

Prep Time

15 Minutes

Cook Time

25 Minutes

Total Time

40 Minutes

Ingredients (Pesto)

  • 1/2 cup toasted Pine Nuts
  • 2 Tablespoons Lemon Juice
  • 1 small Garlic Clove
  • 1/4 teaspoons Seasalt
  • Freshly ground Black Pepper
  • 2 cups Basil Leaves
  • 1/4 cup Extra-Virgin Olive Oil
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan Cheese (optional)

Ingredients (Pasta)

  • 6 ounces of your choice of Pasta
  • Extra Virgin Oilve Oil for drizzling
  • Fresh Lemon Juice, as desired
  • 4 cups Arugula
  • 2 tablespoons Pine Nuts
  • Pinches of red pepper flakes
  • Sea salt and freshly ground Black Pepper
  • Freshly grated Parmesan

Steps

  1. Combine all pesto ingredients in a food processor. Pulse in 5 seconds intervals until a paste forms. Then, gradually add in your olive oil until your pesto forms.
  2. Cook the pasta in a large pot of salted boiling water according to package directions or until al dente. Reserve 1/2 cup of the starchy cooking liquid before draining. Drain the pasta and toss with a tiny bit of olive oil.
  3. In a large skillet over very low heat, add the pesto, 1/4 cup of the reserved pasta water and stir to combine. Add the pasta and toss to coat, and more pasta water as needed to create a loose sauce. The amount of water needed will depend on thickness of your pesto.
  4. Turn the heat off. Add lemon juice, salt, and pepper, to taste. Then, add the arugula and toss to barely wilt. Garnish with the red pepper flakes and pine nuts and serve.
